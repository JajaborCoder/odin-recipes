Recipe of Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

Warm, comforting, and timeless, our Classic Chicken Noodle Soup is the perfect bowl of goodness to soothe your soul. This hearty soup features tender chunks of chicken, perfectly cooked noodles, and a medley of fresh vegetables, all simmered in a rich, flavorful broth. Whether you're feeling under the weather or just craving a cozy meal, this soup is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients:

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Carrots, celery, and onions
  • Garlic
  • Egg noodles
  • Chicken broth
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, thyme, bay leaf)
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper
