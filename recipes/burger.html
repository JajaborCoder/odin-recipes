Recipe of Classic Beef Burger

Description

Sink your teeth into a juicy, flavorful classic beef burger that's perfect for any occasion. This burger is stacked with a perfectly seasoned beef patty, fresh vegetables, and a delicious blend of condiments, all sandwiched between a toasted bun. It's a crowd-pleaser that's easy to make and even easier to love!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (preferably 80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Pickles
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Patties
  2. Cook the Patties
  3. Toast the Buns
  4. Assemble the Burgers
  5. Serve
